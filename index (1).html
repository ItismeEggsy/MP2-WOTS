ABOUT WHEELZ ON THE SOUTH

At Wheelz on the South, we are not just about wheels; we are about a lifestyle—a celebration of the road less traveled, the thrill of the journey, and the pursuit of individuality. Nestled in the heart of Philippines, we are more than a destination for premium wheels and tires; we are an embodiment of passion for automotive excellence.

Founded in 2023, Wheelz on the South was born out of a shared love for automobiles and a desire to redefine the driving experience. What started as a vision has evolved into a haven for enthusiasts seeking more than just standard wheels; we offer an invitation to explore the extraordinary.
